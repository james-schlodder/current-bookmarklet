‚ùì FAQ

Does this work on all websites?

It works on most article and blog websites. Some sites with strict security policies may block it, but it should work on the majority of news sites and blogs.

Is my data safe?

Yes! The bookmarklet only extracts information from the current page you're viewing. Nothing is sent to any server - it all happens in your browser.

Can I customize the extracted fields?

Yes! After extraction, you can edit any field before copying the HTML output.

What browsers are supported?

The bookmarklet works in all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
